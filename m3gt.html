2009 BMW M3 GT

“Designed for racing, the M3 made a strong foundation for a fine-tuned GT model created to outperform the competition in the famous Le Mans championships. This M3 GT roared onto the race circuit from 2009 to 2012, sweeping challengers like Porsche and Ferrari and earning multiple podium finishes.”

“In addition to its impressive aerodynamic body design, it was equipped with a 4-liter V8 engine that could produce 470 horsepower, with a 0-60 mph time of 3.1 seconds. Although this powerful M3 GT was retired after the 2012 season, it made the world take notice of BMW's beloved M3 once again.”
