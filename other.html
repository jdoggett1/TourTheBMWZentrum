Other Vehicles

In addition to the impressive historical models on display in the BMW Zentrum, there are a number of new BMW models showcased as well. This includes an i8, i3, M4 GTS, X-series SUVs, and even motorcycles!

The i8 is BMW’s futuristic, supercar-like sports car. Somewhat surprisingly, the i8 is a plug-in hybrid; this means it has both a gasoline engine and an electric motor. The hybrid setup doesn’t boast mind blowing 0-60 times, but it does yield shockingly good fuel efficiency (up to 134mpg)! Similar to the i8, the i3 is offered in a hybrid model. However, the i3 was originally designed and is still offered as an entirely electric vehicle– BMW’s first zero emissions vehicle! BMW is one of the only automobile manufacturers to make brilliant high-speed performance cars, highly efficient hybrid and electric cars, and magnificent motorcycles!
