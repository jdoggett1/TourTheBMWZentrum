Performance Driving Center

On the other side of the BMW manufacturing plant, right down the road from the BMW Zentrum is the BMW Performance Center. Aside from having a multitude of mean machines on display here, a variety of activities take place at the Performance Center. The Performance Center also often hosts trips to unique, international driving destinations, such as Sweden and Germany.

BMW is one of few automotive manufacturers to host their own driving school. BMW owner or not, anyone is welcome to visit the Performance Center to take wet-driving classes, performance-driving classes, and even off-road driving classes! The excitement of racing around the track can be paired with the equally (if not more) thrilling experience of taking delivery of your new car through the BMW Delivery program where track time is included with the delivery of your vehicle!
